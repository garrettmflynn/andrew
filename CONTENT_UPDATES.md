## Text Spacing Improvements

### Major Typography Changes

**Body Text**:
- Line height: 2.0 (increased from 1.8)
- Creates much more breathing room in paragraphs

**Hero Section**:
- H2 margin-bottom: 2.5rem (from 1.5rem)
- P margin-bottom: 3rem (from 2.5rem)
- P line-height: 2.2 (from default)
- Added hero-tagline and hero-location styles

**Section Headings**:
- H2 margin-bottom: 4.5rem (from 3.5rem)
- H2 line-height: 1.4 (from 1.3)

**Page Content**:
- Line height: 2.2 (from 1.9)
- H2 margin-top: 4.5rem (from 3.5rem)
- H2 margin-bottom: 2rem (from 1.5rem)
- H3 margin-top: 3.5rem (from 2.5rem)
- H3 margin-bottom: 1.5rem (from 1rem)
- P margin-bottom: 2.2rem (from 1.8rem)
- List item margin-bottom: 0.8rem (from 0.6rem)
- List item line-height: 2.0 (from 1.8)

**Service Cards**:
- H3 margin-bottom: 1.5rem (from 1.2rem)
- H3 line-height: 1.4 (added)
- P line-height: 2.0 (from 1.75)

**CTA Section**:
- H2 margin-bottom: 3rem (added)
- P margin-bottom: 2.5rem (from 2rem)
- P line-height: 2.2 (added)
- Added cta-location class with proper spacing

**Item Cards**:
- P line-height: 2.0 (from 1.75)

**Footer**:
- P line-height: 2.2 (from 1.8)
- Footer-bottom p line-height: 2.0 (added)
- Footer-bottom p margin-bottom: 0.5rem (added)

### Result

All text throughout the site now has significantly more breathing room:
- Paragraphs are easier to read with line-height of 2.0-2.2
- Headings have more space above and below them
- List items are less cramped
- Overall feel is more elegant and professional
- Text is easier to scan and digest

The improved spacing creates a more luxurious, editorial feel appropriate for a high-end photography and film production portfolio.
