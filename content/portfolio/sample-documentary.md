## Documentary Project

This documentary project explored the intersection of community and resilience, following several compelling stories over the course of a year.

### Project Details

- **Format**: 4K Digital
- **Role**: Director, Cinematographer, Editor
- **Year**: 2024

### About the Project

The project required careful planning, multiple shooting locations, and extensive interviews. We worked closely with the subjects to ensure their stories were told authentically and respectfully.

- Multiple locations across the region
- 50+ hours of footage
- Intimate interview style
- Natural lighting approach
